The Office Alumni

Michael Scott

Played by Steve Carrell

Michael Scott serves as the regional manager of the Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of the Dunder Mifflin Inc. paper company. His character is lovable, despite being shallow, ignorant, and unaware of basic social norms. While his jokes and comments are oftentimes offensive and insensitive, he usually means well and has a big heart.

Steve Carrell is an American actor and comedian most known for his roles in The Office, Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy, The 40-Year-Old Virgin, and Evan Almighty. Most recently, he is known for voicing Gru in Despiscable Me.

Dwight Schrute

Played by Rainn Wilson

Dwight Schrute is one of the highest-ranking salesman at the Scranton branch of Dunder Mifflin and is also the original assistant to the regional manager. He also is the proprieter of Schrute Farms which is known for its beet production. Dwight acts superior to his peers and can be very intense and serious, but finds himself to be the butt of many jokes due to his gullible, ignorant, and naive nature.

Rainn Wilson is an American actor, comedian, writer, director, and producer. He is most known for his lead roles in The Office, The Rocker, and Super as well as supporting roles in horror films such as Cooties and The Boy.

Pam Beesley Halpert

Played by Jenna Fischer

Pam Beesley Halpert begins as the receptionist at Dunder Mifflin and eventually goes on to become a saleswoman and then office administrator. At first, her character is shy and unsure but eventually gains confidence and becomes assertive.

Jenna Fischer is an American actress,= most known for her role in The Office and has appeared in films such as Blades of Glory, The Promotion, and Hall Pass. She currently is the co-host of the podcast Office Ladies alongside her co-star Angela Kinsey.

Jim Halpert

Played by John Krasinski

Jim Halpert is a sales representative at Dunder Mifflin and has been described as the intelligent, mild-mannered straight man. He is also constantly pulling pranks on his coworker, Dwight Schuute.

John Krasinski is an American actor and filmmaker most known for his roles in The Office, 13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers of Benghazi, A Quiet Place, and Jack Ryan.

Angela Martin

Played by Angela Kinsey

Angela Martin is the uptight head accountant at Dunder Mifflin. She is cold, stern, and obsessed with cats.

Angela Kinsey is an American actress most known for her roles in The Office, A.P. Bio, and panelist on MTV's Delicoiusness. She is also co-host of the podcast Office Ladies alongside her co-star Jenna Fischer.

Kevin Malone

Played by Brian Baumgartner

Kevin Malone is an accountant at Dunder Mifflin and his known for his lack of intelligence and communication skills, as well as his clumsiness.

Brian Baumgartner is most known for his role in The Office.

Oscar Martinez

Played by Oscar Nuñez

Oscar Martinez is an account at Dunder Mifflin and is known for being rational, efficient, and intelligent and is the butt of many of Michael Scott and Kevin Malone's insensitive jokes due to his Mexican heritage and his sexuality.

Oscar Nuñez is a Cuban-American actor best known for his role in The Office.

Stanley Hudson

Played by Leslie David Baker

Stanely Hudson is the longest tenured salesman at Dunder Mifflin and known for his strong, silent personality as well as his general grumpiness.

Leslie David Baker is an American actor best known for his role in The Office.

Meredith Palmer

Played by Kate Flannery

Meredith Palmer is the supplier relations representative at Dunder Mifflin and is most known for being socially inappropriate and sexually promiscuous.

Kate Flannery is an American actress best known for her role in The Office and her early theatre work.

Creed Bratton

Played by Creed Bratton

Creed Bratton is the elderly, eccentric quality assurance representative for Dunder Mifflin who has a shady past and often participates in suspicious activities. He often forgets his coworkers' names and his job responsibilites and tells outlandish stories that may or may not be true.

Creed Bratton is an American actor, singer, and musician. He was a former member of the rock band the Grass Roots and is most known for his role in The Office.

Kelly Kapoor

Played by Mindy Kaling

Kelly Kapoor is the customer service representative at Dunder Mifflin who is known for being talkative and having a similar personality to a stereotypical teenage girl such as boys, dating, and celebrity gossip. She is also oftentimes the butt of Michael Scott's racially charged jokes.

Mindy Kaling is an American actress, comedian, screenwriter, and producer most known for her roles in The Office, The Mindy Project, Never Have I Ever, and The Sex Lives of College Girls.

Phyllis Vance

Played by Phyllis Smith

Phyllis Vance is a sales representative at Dunder Mifflin known for being quiet, friendly, and a fan of girl talk and gossip.

Phyllis Smith is an American actress and casting associate best known for her roles in The Office and Inside Out.

Darryl Philbin

Played by Craig Robinson

Darryl Philbin is the foreman of the Dunder Mifflin warehouse known for being laid back, innovative, funny, and willing to take advantage of Michael Scott's naïveté.

Craig Robinson is an American actor and comedian best known for his roles in The Office, The Cleveland Show, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Pineapple Express, Zack and Miri Make a Porno, Hot Tub Time Machine, and This Is The End.

Ryan Howard

Played by B.J. Novak

Ryan Howard was a receptionist then salesman then Vice President of Sales then convicted fraudster at Dunder Mifflin. He is known for being erratic and two-faced.

B.J. Novak is an American actor, comedian, director, producer, screenwriter, and author most known for his roles in The Office, The Mindy Project, The Amazing Spider-Man 2, Inglourious Basterds, Saving Mr. Banks, and Vengeance.

Erin Hannon

Played by Ellie Kemper

Erin Hannon is the office receptionist at Dunder Mifflin most known for her lack of intelligence as well as her cheerful, silly demeanor, and childlike naïveté.

Ellie Kemper is an American actress and comedian best known for her roles in The Office, Unbreakable, Bridesmaids, 21 Jump Street, Sex Tape, and Home Sweet Home Alone.

Andy Bernard

Played by Ed Helms

Andy Bernard is the Regional Director in Charge of Sales at Dunder Mifflin Stamford but later becomes the regional manager at Scranton. He is known for being clueless, obnoxious, but never malicious, and for talking endlessly about his Ivy League education. He is also tone-deaf on issues of class and race.

Ed Helms is an American actor, comedian, singer, writer, and producer most known for his roles in The Office, The Daily Show with Jon Stewart, The Hangover trilogy and Tag.

Toby Flenderson

Played by Paul Lieberstein

Toby Flenderson was the human resources representative at Dunder Mifflin. He is known for being soft-spoken, mild-mannered, and ill-fortuned. Michael Scott views Toby as the bane of his existence and has a deep hatred for him.

Paul Lieberstein is an American actor, writer and executive producer most known for his roles in The Office, The Newsroom, Ghosted, and Song of Back and Neck.
